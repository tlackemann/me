Who Is Tommy?

Tom began programming when he was 12-years-old. Being self-taught, he found every chance to pick up some of the web's latest technologies and implement them into a vast array of projects ranging from personal blogs to full-featured social networks. His entrepreneural attitude and excitement for new opportunities lead him to work for Pixafy where he happily hacks away at the latest and greatest ventures. Tom grew up on Long Island, spends his free time with his beast of a golden retriever, Riley, and has a slight obsession with good craft beers.
